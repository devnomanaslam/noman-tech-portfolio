## What Are Office Add-ins?

Office Add-ins are small applications that **run inside Office apps** and extend their functionality. They allow you to:

- Automate repetitive tasks
- Pull data from external APIs
- Customize the Office UI
- Enhance collaboration across teams

For example, a custom **Outlook add-in** could automatically format emails, schedule meetings, or integrate CRM data directly into the email interface.

## Technologies You Need

Building Office Add-ins requires a mix of **frontend skills and Microsoft APIs**:

- **Office.js API**: The main JavaScript library to interact with Office apps
- **HTML, CSS, JavaScript**: For building the UI
- **React or Angular**: For dynamic, modern add-ins
- **REST APIs**: For integrating with external data sources
